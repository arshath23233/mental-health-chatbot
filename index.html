About MindSpace

A Mental Health Journal Chatbot — Final Year Project

Our Mission

MindSpace is designed to provide a safe, non-judgmental space for individuals to express their thoughts and feelings. Through empathetic conversations, it helps users develop self-awareness, track emotional patterns, and build healthier coping mechanisms.

How It Works

  • Empathetic Chatbot — Uses advanced NLP-inspired response generation with contextual understanding, active listening techniques, and therapeutic conversation patterns
  • Automatic Journaling — Every conversation is saved as a journal entry with mood detection and emotion tagging
  • Crisis Detection — Real-time keyword monitoring triggers immediate display of Indian crisis helplines
  • Mood Analytics — Visual dashboard tracks emotional trends over time

Privacy & Safety

Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties. MindSpace is not a replacement for professional mental health care. If you are in crisis, please contact the helplines listed in this application.

Key Features

Human-like empathetic responses
Context-aware conversations
Automatic mood detection
Journal entry auto-saving
Crisis keyword detection
Indian crisis helplines
Mood tracking dashboard
Conversation export

⚠️ Disclaimer: MindSpace is an academic project and should not be considered a substitute for professional mental health treatment. Always seek help from a qualified mental health professional if needed.
